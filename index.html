Liability Waiver & Assumption of Risk

Inherent risk. Swinging a golf club inside a simulator enclosure carries inherent risks, including but not limited to bodily injury from clubs, balls, screens, or other participants; trip-and-fall hazards; eye, hand, or back strain; and property damage. By participating, each player acknowledges and assumes these risks.

Safety rules. Only one player may occupy the swing zone at a time. Spectators must remain behind the marked safe-zone line. No alcohol consumption is permitted while swinging. Closed-toe shoes are recommended. The on-site attendant has final authority to pause play for safety.

Release.

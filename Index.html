Who We Are

The Golf Experience, Delivered.

Par-Tee Golf is a mobile golf simulator rental company bringing the thrill of the course directly to your event. Our cutting-edge simulators let you enjoy golf anywhere — backyard, ballroom, or boardroom.

We bring the full setup: advanced simulator, premium equipment, and personalized settings. Our on-site team handles everything so you can focus on having a great time. Whether you're a scratch golfer or have never swung a club, Par-Tee Golf is for everyone.

FAQ

Common Questions

How much space do I need? +
We need approximately 15ft × 18ft of clear space to set up the simulator, giving players room to swing freely and spectators room to watch. We also need access to a standard 120V power outlet.
Can guests bring their own clubs? +
Absolutely! Par-Tee Golf is compatible with all standard golf clubs. We also provide a right-handed set with every rental, plus optional left-handed, ladies', and kids' sets available upon request.
Do guests need golf experience? +
Not at all! Our on-site attendant is there to help guests of all skill levels get set up and enjoy themselves. Beginners love it just as much as avid golfers.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date. For peak seasons (spring and fall) or large events, 4–6 weeks is ideal. We do accommodate last-minute requests when possible.
Can you set up outdoors? +
Yes! We can set up indoors or outdoors as long as conditions are suitable (no rain or strong wind). We'll work with you to find the best setup for your venue. A covered outdoor space is ideal.
What courses are available? +
We use GSPro software, which features 100+ meticulously designed real-world golf courses including Augusta National, Pebble Beach, St Andrews, and many more. Guests can choose from a wide variety.
Do you offer custom packages? +
Yes! We offer fully flexible packages. Need a half-day, multi-simulator setup, or something totally custom? Just reach out via our booking form or call us and we'll put something together for you.
What's your cancellation policy? +
We understand plans change. Contact us as soon as possible if you need to reschedule or cancel. We'll work with you to find a solution — full details are included in your booking confirmation.

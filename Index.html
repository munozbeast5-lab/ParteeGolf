FAQ

Common Questions

How much space do I need? +
We need approximately 15ft × 18ft of clear space to set up the simulator, giving players room to swing freely and spectators room to watch. We also need access to a standard 120V power outlet.
Can guests bring their own clubs? +
Absolutely! Par-Tee Golf is compatible with all standard golf clubs. We also provide a right-handed set with every rental, plus optional left-handed, ladies', and kids' sets available upon request.
Do guests need golf experience? +
Not at all! Our on-site attendant is there to help guests of all skill levels get set up and enjoy themselves. Beginners love it just as much as avid golfers.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date. For peak seasons (spring and fall) or large events, 4–6 weeks is ideal. We do accommodate last-minute requests when possible.
Can you set up outdoors? +
Yes! Our simulator works great outdoors. We do recommend a shaded or tented area for the best screen visibility. We'll work with you to find the ideal setup location at your venue.
What's included in every package? +
Every package includes delivery, full setup and takedown, a professional on-site attendant, a right-handed club set, HD projection, and access to 100+ courses via GSPro software.
Do you serve the entire Dallas-Fort Worth area? +
We serve the greater DFW metroplex and surrounding areas. Travel fees may apply for locations beyond a 50-mile radius. Contact us for availability in your area.
What happens if it rains? +
For outdoor events, we monitor weather closely and will work with you on contingency plans. If the event needs to be rescheduled due to severe weather, we'll accommodate at no extra charge.
